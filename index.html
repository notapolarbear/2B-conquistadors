Instructions

Welcome to Conquistadors, a collector and story mode game!


Your goal while playing this game is to collect and build as many things

as you possibly can. You can obtain metal, food, water, oxygen tanks, wires,

minerals, and computer chips when you press the "scavenge" button.


To construct a gas compounder, you need 10 metal, 15 wires, 2 oxygen

tanks, and 11 minerals. To craft a drill, you need 10 metal. To make a water

filter, you need 10 minerals, 3 mesh, and 1 drill. To create an automated

farm, you need 10 mesh, 2 computer chips, 1 well, 1 solar panel, and 5 wires.

To fabricate a blue printer, you need 10 metal, 20 wires, 1 solar panel, and 5

computer chips. To scramble mesh, you need 3 metal and 4 wires. To fashion

a solar panel, you need 10 minerals, 3 mesh, and 1 drill.


Have a good time playing!
